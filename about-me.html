About Me

My name is Beheshta Azimi. I was born in Kabul in February 2006 and currently live in Germany. I am deeply passionate about education, technology, and community work.
I come from a supportive family that values education and personal growth, which has shaped my dreams and identity.

I am currently studying German and preparing to pursue a degree in Software Engineering. In the future, I aspire to work with global organizations like the UN or Google. My journey has not been easy, but every challenge has helped me grow stronger and more determined.
